// how it works

30 seconds to set up.

No bloated onboarding. No friend invites. Just the math.

  1. Add the first day of her last period. That's it.
  2. The app figures out where she is in the cycle.
  3. Every day, you get one specific move tuned to her phase + her profile.
  4. When something's happening right now, tap a situation chip. Phase-aware moves + exact phrases.
  5. End of the cycle: a one-page Receipt of how you actually showed up.
// what it isn't

Some honesty up front.

Not a doctor.

This isn't medical advice. PMDD, endometriosis, anything severe — that's a real conversation with a real provider.

Not a tracker for her.

This is for you. She has Clue, Flo, whatever she likes. This is the coach in your pocket.

Not data collection.

No account, no server, no tracking. Everything lives in your phone's local storage. Export or wipe whenever.
