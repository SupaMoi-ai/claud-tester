BC BRO CODE · privacy

YOUR DATA STAYS ON YOUR PHONE.

// the short version

BRO CODE stores everything in this browser's local storage on this device. No login. No accounts. No tracking. No analytics. Your data never leaves your phone.

If you clear your browser data, it's gone. If you switch phones, use Export in Her Profile to save a JSON file, then Import on the new device.

What we store

  • Her profile — name, cycle length, last period, any optional fields you fill in.
  • Your logs — what you've logged (gift / talk / conflict / apology / listened).
  • Your settings — reminder slots, the accordions you've opened, your install nudge state.

What we don't do

  • No account creation. No email required.
  • No tracking pixels. No telemetry. No analytics scripts.
  • No third-party SDKs, no ad networks, no fingerprinting.
  • No data sent to any server. The app loads, then runs entirely on your device.

Notifications

If you enable any of the three reminder slots, the browser fires them locally from a timer on your phone. The notification content never leaves your device. Push permissions are managed by your browser's notification settings.

Calendar export

The Add to calendar buttons generate a .ics file that downloads to your device. You decide whether to add it to your system calendar. Nothing is uploaded.

// if you found this on his phone

This is a tool for him, not surveillance of you. Cycle data is entered by him; nothing is sent anywhere. He's trying to be a better partner — that's the whole point. If you'd rather he didn't use it, tell him. He'll listen.
